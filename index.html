    About Us

    We are two college students passionate about helping our peers succeed. This platform is built for students, by students — to make access to study materials, notes, and assignments simple, fast, and organized.

    Whether you're preparing for exams, looking for previous year papers, or just want well-structured notes, we've got your back.

    Key Features

    • 📚 Downloadable Notes: Subject-wise, well-organized notes to help you study smarter.
    • 📝 Assignments: Access and submit assignments easily with proper deadlines.
    • 📅 Academic Calendar: Stay updated with test dates, project deadlines, and events.
    • 💬 Community Chat: Discuss doubts, share tips, and connect with your classmates.
    • 📤 Contribution Option: Upload your own notes and help others too!
